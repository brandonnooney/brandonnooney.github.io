What is jackal?

The founders of Jackal Muay Thai set out to train as many individuals as possible in the art of Muay Thai in Thailand using only authentic methods. As the camp has grown, it has become a place where fans and professional athletes from across the world can come to relax and recharge.

It doesn't matter whether you're on vacation, a fitness fanatic or a member of the military; we accept all levels of fitness and fighting experience.

Over the past few years, the Camp has swiftly become a training ground for the world's best and brightest athletes. Many documentaries, travel shows, and other media outlets have showcased jackal's Muay Thai, MMA, and fitness training, giving it a well-deserved reputation as Thailand's best.
