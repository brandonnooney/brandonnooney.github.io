KICKBOXING

Kickboxing is a category of stand-up combat sports and a type of boxing that uses kicking and punching as its primary weapons. The fight is held in a boxing ring, with participants wearing boxing gloves, mouthguards, shorts, and bare feet to encourage kicks. Kickboxing is a sport that can be used for self-defense, exercise, or competition.

 

MMA

Mixed Martial Arts (MMA), also known as cage fighting, no holds barred, and ultimate fighting, is a full-contact combat sport that combines striking, grappling, and ground fighting tactics from diverse combat sports from across the world.

 

BOXING

Boxing is a combat sport in which two participants in a boxing ring throw punches at each other for a predetermined amount of time while wearing protective gloves and other protective equipment such as hand wraps and mouthguards.
